### The F1 Score:
The F1 score is the harmonic mean of precision and recall, taking both metrics into account by using their product divided by their sum. It is particularly useful when the costs of false positives and false negatives are very different. The formula for the F1 score is:

$ \text{Precision} = \frac{\text{True Positives}}{\text{True Positives} + \text{False Positives}} $

$ \text{Recall} = \frac{\text{True Positives}}{\text{True Positives} + \text{False Negatives}} $

$ \text{F1 Score} = 2 \times \frac{\text{Precision} \times \text{Recall}}{\text{Precision} + \text{Recall}} $
